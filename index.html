Research Focus

Six Core Pillars

The thematic areas that define our work at the frontier of GeoAI

🛰️

Autonomous GeoAI & AI4Science

Building agent-based systems that can autonomously process, reason, and act on geospatial data to advance scientific discovery.

🌍

Foundation Models for Earth Observation

Adapting and evaluating large pretrained models for satellite imagery, land cover mapping, and environmental monitoring tasks.

🗺️

Multimodal Spatial Reasoning

Fusing satellite imagery, street-view photos, maps, and temporal sequences for richer geospatial understanding.

🔬

GIScience & Spatial Intelligence

Advancing spatial thinking through GWR, spatial query, and AI-augmented geographic information systems.

🏙️

Urban & Disaster Intelligence

Street-view AI for damage assessment, urban building analytics, and human-centric sensing of built environments.

⚖️

Explainable & Responsible GeoAI

Open science practices, reproducibility, and trustworthy deployment of AI systems for spatial and environmental applications.
